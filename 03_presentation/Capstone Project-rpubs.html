Swiftkey Data Product Introduction

Chuong Hoang
Apr 05, 2016

The presentation is for introducing a Swiftkey data product that enable to predict the next word that user intends to type. The data product is the capstone project for the Coursera Data Science specialization provided by the Johns Hopkins University

Objectives of the Application

There are 4 main objectives of this Swiftkey Predictive Apllication are:

  1. Leverage Social English Datasets (blogs, news, twitter;~556 MB) for terms frequency study
  2. The predictive algorithm have to make sure near-realtime respone, with responding time must lower than 1 second
  3. The application has to maintain two responding approaches: with and without English Stop Word.
  4. Not only provide a predicted word (most frequent appeared word following user input terms), the application also return other possible options ranking by n-gram and its frequency.
